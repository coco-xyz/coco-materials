# Singapore Building Materials Distributor × COCO AI

> 200 messages a day drowning in WhatsApp. Manual invoices capped at 30 per day. QS quotes taking a week. COCO AI changed all three.

## Customer Background

This company is a Singapore-based building materials distributor serving construction contractors and engineering firms, with a product range spanning structural materials, finishing materials, and specialty items. Customer communication is primarily conducted over WhatsApp and WeCom (企业微信), with the business handling a high volume of inquiry, quotation, order confirmation, and invoicing activity during peak periods.

## Challenges

- **Severe message backlog:** 200+ WhatsApp messages arriving daily, with manual review making it easy to miss orders — each missed inquiry a direct revenue loss
- **Invoice processing bottleneck:** Manual data entry limited invoice throughput to approximately 30 per day, falling well short of peak-season demand and delaying client billing cycles
- **High QS outsourcing costs and slow turnaround:** Every quantity surveying (QS) estimate required engaging an external Quantity Surveyor at SGD 3,000–5,000 per engagement, with a wait time of 5–7 working days — critically undermining the company's ability to respond to RFQs competitively

## COCO Solution

COCO built an integrated AI capability layer covering three core operational areas: front-end customer communication, invoice processing, and construction quantity estimation.

### WhatsApp + WeCom Intent Recognition

- The AI monitors incoming messages across WhatsApp and WeCom in real time, automatically classifying each message by intent: price inquiry, order placement, order follow-up, or after-sales support
- Standard queries (stock availability, pricing, lead times) are answered directly by the AI; complex requests are flagged and assigned to the relevant team member
- Every message gets a response — near-zero missed orders

### OCR Invoice Processing

- Customer-submitted invoices (images or PDFs) are processed by AI OCR, which extracts key fields — item descriptions, quantities, unit prices, totals — automatically
- Structured data flows directly into downstream systems; single-invoice processing time drops from ~10 minutes of manual entry to seconds, with no daily throughput ceiling

### Multimodal Quantity Estimation

- Customers upload engineering drawings, site photos, or text descriptions; the AI performs multimodal analysis and automatically produces material quantity estimates and preliminary cost breakdowns
- Replaces the previous external QS outsourcing workflow entirely

## Key Results

- **Near-zero missed orders** from WhatsApp — all incoming inquiries captured and responded to
- Invoice processing efficiency **multiplied**; daily throughput ceiling eliminated, peak-season backlogs resolved
- QS turnaround compressed from **5–7 working days to hours**
- Per-engagement QS outsourcing costs of **SGD 3,000–5,000 eliminated**, with significant annualized savings
- Operations team can focus on complex project follow-up and client relationship management

## Key Insight

> "In building materials, the competition is straightforward: whoever quotes first, wins the order. COCO moved us from the back of the pack to the front on response speed."
