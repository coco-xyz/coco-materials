# Leading Container Shipping Group × COCO AI

> 10+ AI employees deployed. No visibility into what any of them were doing. COCO Workspace broke open the black box and turned isolated agents into a collaboration network.

## Customer Background

This group is a leading container shipping enterprise in Southeast Asia, with operations spanning multiple countries and functions including shipping agency, port coordination, customer service, and documentation processing. The group had already deployed over 10 AI employees across different platforms, giving it a meaningful foundation of AI adoption. However, as the deployment footprint grew, management and coordination challenges emerged at the organizational layer.

## Challenges

- **The AI black box:** Management had no real-time visibility into what each AI employee was working on, what tasks were completed, or where bottlenecks existed — no unified operational view
- **Cross-organizational barriers:** Information flow between departments and between countries depended on human relays; AI agents had no ability to collaborate across organizational boundaries
- **No agent-to-agent communication:** Each AI employee operated independently with no mechanism to hand off tasks or share outputs with another agent — creating a new layer of silos on top of the old ones
- **Capability ceiling at Q&A:** Existing AI employees were primarily used for information lookup and question answering, without the ability to proactively trigger or execute complex multi-step workflows

## COCO Solution

COCO deployed the **COCO Workspace** collaboration layer on top of the group's existing AI employees, adding cross-organizational connectivity and an agent collaboration network:

### Cross-Organizational Connectivity

- Established authorized information channels across country subsidiaries and partner organizations, enabling AI employees to retrieve and route information across organizational boundaries
- Eliminated the "information to human, human to relay" intermediate step — enabling direct, automated routing

### Agent Collaboration Network

- Built an inter-agent communication protocol allowing one AI employee to automatically pass results or subsequent tasks to another upon completion
- Complex multi-step business processes (booking confirmation → document generation → customer notification) now flow automatically through the agent network without human touchpoints at each stage

### CEO Digital Dashboard

- A unified real-time dashboard displays the working status, task volumes, completion rates, and key business metrics for all AI employees
- Management has full visibility into the AI collaboration network's operational health at any time — the black box is gone

## Key Results

- Cross-border business routing compressed from **10+ minutes to seconds**, dramatically accelerating multi-country coordination
- AI employee capabilities upgraded from "responsive Q&A" to **end-to-end workflow automation**
- Management achieved real-time visibility into AI team operations for the first time, enabling data-grounded decisions
- With the multi-agent collaboration network in place, overall automation coverage increased significantly and manual touchpoints were reduced

## Key Insight

> "When you have more than 10 AI employees, managing those agents becomes a new operational problem in itself. COCO Workspace addresses the collaboration and governance layer that emerges at AI scale."
