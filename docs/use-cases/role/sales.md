# Sales Use Cases

AI employees for SDRs, account executives, and sales teams.

## AI Lead Researcher
**Pain:** SDRs manually search LinkedIn — max 20 leads/day with incomplete data.
**Solution:** AI scrapes, enriches, and scores leads from multiple databases automatically.
**Result:** 20 → 200 leads/day. Pipeline never runs dry.

## AI CRM Updater
**Pain:** AEs waste 45 minutes daily on CRM data entry. CRM accuracy at 60%.
**Solution:** AI auto-logs calls, emails, meetings. Fields auto-populated, timeline updated real-time.
**Result:** 45min/day → 0. CRM accuracy: 60% → 100%.

## AI Proposal Generator
**Pain:** Custom sales proposals take 3+ hours each.
**Solution:** AI pulls client data, selects case studies, generates tailored proposal in 15 minutes.
**Result:** 3 hours → 15 minutes. More proposals = more deals.

## AI Follow-up Writer
**Pain:** Writing personalized follow-ups takes 30 minutes after each meeting.
**Solution:** AI drafts personalized emails referencing specific discussion points in 2 minutes.
**Result:** 30min → 2min. Reply rate: 35% → 52%.

## AI Client Research Brief
**Pain:** Prepping for sales meetings = 1 hour Googling company news and org charts.
**Solution:** AI compiles full dossier in 5 minutes: company overview, key people, recent news, talking points.
**Result:** 1 hour → 5 minutes. Walk into every meeting prepared.

## AI Quote Calculator
**Pain:** Custom pricing quotes take 2+ hours with discount rules and approvals.
**Solution:** AI applies pricing rules, checks approval thresholds, generates professional quote in 10 minutes.
**Result:** 2 hours → 10 minutes. Faster quotes = higher win rates.
