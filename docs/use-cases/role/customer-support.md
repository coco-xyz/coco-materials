# Customer Support Use Cases

AI employees for support teams and customer success.

## AI Ticket Classifier
**Pain:** 500 tickets/day, 5 minutes each to read, categorize, and route. 40+ hours/week on triage.
**Solution:** AI instantly classifies by type, urgency, and routes to correct team.
**Result:** Triage: 5min → instant. Misroute rate: 15% → <1%. First response 5x faster.

## AI Knowledge Base Builder
**Pain:** Same 20 questions = 60% of tickets. Nobody has time to write help articles.
**Solution:** AI analyzes resolved tickets, clusters patterns, generates 50 polished FAQ articles in 1 hour.
**Result:** Self-service: 20% → 65%. Ticket volume down 40%.

## AI Multi-Language Support
**Pain:** Customers speak 15 languages. Support team speaks 2.
**Solution:** AI detects language, understands the issue, responds in fluent native text. 15+ languages simultaneously.
**Result:** 5 translators → 1 AI. Response: 12h → 8min. Satisfaction 90%+ in every language.

## AI VIP Escalation
**Pain:** $2M client's ticket buried at #147 in queue. 30% of VIP issues get missed.
**Solution:** AI cross-references CRM, detects high-value accounts, auto-escalates with full context.
**Result:** VIP missed: 30% → 0%. Response time for top accounts under 15 minutes.
