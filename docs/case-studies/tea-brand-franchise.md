# Leading Tea Chain × COCO AI

> 47+ unanswered franchise inquiries every night. After deploying COCO AI: 7×24 multilingual coverage, 60–80% of queries auto-resolved.

## Customer Background

This brand is one of the world's largest bubble tea chains, operating approximately 60,000 stores globally with over 4,000 locations across Southeast Asia. The franchise model accounts for roughly 99% of the store network, making franchise recruitment and franchisee support two of the most operationally critical functions in the business.

## Challenges

- **Nightly inquiry backlog:** An average of 47+ franchise inquiry messages went unanswered every night, only to be reviewed the following morning — costing warm leads by the time anyone responded
- **Multilingual complexity:** Southeast Asia's diverse language landscape (Thai, Vietnamese, Bahasa Malay, Bahasa Indonesia, English, and more) made full human coverage impractical
- **High volume of repetitive queries:** The majority of inquiries focused on franchise fees, eligibility requirements, and site selection criteria — standard questions that consumed significant staff time
- **Dispersed operational knowledge:** Store staff frequently needed to contact multiple departments or search through manuals to resolve day-to-day issues
- **Manual back-office load:** Cross-market data consolidation, reporting, and internal coordination relied entirely on manual effort

## COCO Solution

COCO deployed **3 AI employees** to cover franchise front-end, store operations, and back-office automation:

### Franchise Inquiry AI (Customer-Facing)

- Available 7×24 across WhatsApp, LINE, and the brand's official website
- Real-time language switching across all major Southeast Asian languages — no human switchover required
- Automatically identifies inquiry intent and provides direct answers for standard questions on franchise policy, fees, and location requirements
- High-intent leads are automatically tagged and escalated to the human sales team for follow-up

### Store Operations Knowledge Base AI (Internal)

- Consolidates brand-wide SOPs, product manuals, and emergency handling procedures into a structured, searchable knowledge base
- Store staff get accurate answers in seconds without needing to escalate to headquarters
- Supports queries in Mandarin, English, and major Southeast Asian languages

### Back-Office Automation AI

- Automatically aggregates multi-market data and generates standardized operational reports
- Streamlines cross-market internal communications with automated information extraction and task tracking

## Key Results

- **60–80%** of standard franchise inquiries resolved automatically, with no human involvement required
- **~10 FTE** freed from repetitive inquiry handling, redeployed to strategic business development
- **7×24 coverage** across Southeast Asia's multilingual markets — the nightly backlog problem eliminated entirely
- Response time to high-intent franchise prospects reduced from next-day to **real-time**, significantly tightening the conversion funnel

## Key Insight

> "For a franchise brand at scale, the biggest hidden cost is the potential franchisee whose message went unanswered. AI employees don't just save labor — they make sure every opportunity gets caught."
