# Operations Use Cases

AI employees for operations teams, project managers, and supply chain.

## AI Meeting Notes
**Pain:** 30 minutes after every meeting writing notes. Action items get lost by Friday.
**Solution:** AI records, transcribes, summarizes, extracts action items with owners and deadlines. Distributed instantly.
**Result:** 30min post-meeting → instant. Action item completion: 43% → 91%.

## AI Inventory Forecaster
**Pain:** Weekly manual forecasting misses trends. Stockouts and overstock cost thousands.
**Solution:** AI analyzes sales, seasons, and supplier lead times continuously. Reorder alerts before you run out.
**Result:** Stockouts: -62%. Dead stock: -40%. Inventory turnover: +35%.

## AI Vendor Evaluator
**Pain:** Evaluating 5 vendor proposals = 1 week of reading contracts and building spreadsheets.
**Solution:** AI analyzes all proposals, compares terms and pricing, checks risks, generates ranked recommendation.
**Result:** 1 week → 2 hours. Same rigor, 20x faster.
