# Financial RegTech Company × COCO AI

> They sold AI compliance platforms to financial institutions. Their own operations were still manual. COCO AI changed that.

## Customer Background

This company is a financial compliance technology firm whose core product is an AI-powered regulatory monitoring and risk control platform. Its client base includes banks, asset management firms, and fintech companies across Asia-Pacific and the Middle East. The firm has deep conviction in AI's value for financial regulation — and actively advocates AI-driven operations to its clients — but its own internal operations told a different story.

## Challenges

- **Manual regulatory push:** Regulatory updates are frequent and span multiple jurisdictions across Asia-Pacific and the Middle East. The process of monitoring, summarizing, and distributing relevant regulatory changes to clients was slow, labor-intensive, and prone to gaps
- **Three disconnected systems:** Marketing, sales, and customer service each operated on separate platforms. Client data couldn't be shared across functions — the same client was effectively a stranger to each department
- **No unified customer view:** Without a consolidated client profile, sales lacked the full context to conduct targeted outreach, and customer service couldn't anticipate client needs proactively
- **Weak proactive outreach capability:** The sales team had no systematic mechanism for proactive client engagement; demand was largely captured reactively, waiting for clients to reach out

## COCO Solution

COCO deployed **3 AI employees** and integrated the company's three previously siloed systems into a unified platform:

### Operations AI (Automated Regulatory Push)

- Monitors regulatory authority publications and updates across Asia-Pacific and Middle East target markets in real time
- Automatically extracts, summarizes, and formats new regulations and guidance notes
- Generates tailored regulatory briefs for each client based on their business type and jurisdictions, and delivers them proactively
- Regulatory coverage is complete and timely — from "manually compiled a few days later" to "auto-delivered same day"

### Sales AI (Proactive Outreach)

- Working from the unified client view, the AI automatically identifies high-intent prospects and clients at renewal risk
- Initiates personalized outreach via email and messaging channels, delivering curated compliance insights and relevant product updates
- Automates lead nurturing and initial follow-up, freeing human sales to focus on deepening high-value opportunities

### Knowledge Base AI + CRM Unification

- Integrates the three previously separate systems into a unified client data hub and compliance knowledge base
- Customer service AI can access full client history and compliance knowledge in real time, delivering accurate and consistent responses
- New team members onboard faster with access to structured product documentation and client context from day one

## Key Results

- Regulatory push fully automated, covering **all target markets across Asia-Pacific and the Middle East** — no gaps, no delays
- Three separate systems unified into a single customer view; **client data connected end-to-end for the first time**
- Proactive sales outreach systematized; lead conversion efficiency improved
- The company now operates the way it advises clients to — AI-driven from the inside out

## Key Insight

> "We were selling AI compliance solutions to financial institutions while running our own operations manually. That contradiction bothered us. Now we can genuinely say: this is how we operate too."
