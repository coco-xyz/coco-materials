# Process Automation Use Cases

AI employees that automate repetitive workflows.

| Use Case | Role | What It Does | Metric |
|----------|------|-------------|--------|
| AI Ticket Classifier | Support | Auto-classifies and routes support tickets | 5min/ticket → instant |
| AI Expense Auditor | Finance | Auto-reviews expense reports against policy | 20min/report → instant |
| AI CRM Updater | Sales | Auto-logs all sales activities to CRM | 45min/day → 0 |
| AI Invoice Processor | Finance | Extracts data, matches POs, routes invoices | 15min → 30sec |
| AI Interview Scheduler | HR | Coordinates calendars and sends invites | 45min → 3min |
| AI Meeting Notes | Operations | Records, summarizes, distributes action items | 30min → instant |
| AI Deploy Monitor | DevOps | Monitors deployments, auto-rollbacks on issues | Manual → auto |
| AI Resume Screener | HR | Batch-scores resumes against job requirements | 3 days → 2 hours |
| AI Quote Calculator | Sales | Generates pricing quotes with discount rules | 2h → 10min |
