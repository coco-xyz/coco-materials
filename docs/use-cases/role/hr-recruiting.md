# HR / Recruiting Use Cases

AI employees for HR teams, recruiters, and people operations.

## AI Resume Screener
**Pain:** 500 resumes per role. 3 days of manual reading. Top candidates leave during the wait.
**Solution:** AI reads every resume, scores against requirements, shortlists Top 20 with written reasoning.
**Result:** 3 days → 2 hours. Hiring cycle: 21 days → 9 days.

## AI Job Description Writer
**Pain:** Writing one good JD takes 2+ hours of wordsmithing and bias-checking.
**Solution:** AI generates inclusive, SEO-optimized JDs from a brief set of requirements in 10 minutes.
**Result:** 2 hours → 10 minutes. Better listings = more qualified applicants.

## AI Interview Scheduler
**Pain:** Coordinating times for candidate + 4-person panel = 45 minutes of calendar Tetris per candidate.
**Solution:** AI checks all calendars, finds optimal slots, sends invites, handles rescheduling.
**Result:** 45min → 3min per candidate. 22 hours/week saved.

## AI Onboarding Assistant
**Pain:** New hires take 2 weeks to feel productive. Half their questions go unanswered.
**Solution:** AI creates personalized onboarding plans and answers questions 24/7.
**Result:** 2 weeks → 3 days to full ramp. HR saves 10 hours per person.
