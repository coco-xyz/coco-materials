# Top FMCG Company × COCO AI

> 5–6 Lark groups, 10+ markets, multilingual chaos. COCO AI took over the coordination layer so critical tasks no longer sink into the message stream.

## Customer Background

This company is the Southeast Asian regional operating entity of a global top-tier FMCG group, with business operations spanning more than 10 markets across the region. Teams use Lark as their primary collaboration platform. Given the diversity of local languages, time zones, and business rhythms across markets, cross-team promotional coordination had long been a significant bottleneck for operational efficiency.

## Challenges

- **Fragmented group communication:** Promotional campaign approvals were distributed across 5–6 separate Lark groups, with critical information frequently buried under high message volumes
- **Multilingual coordination costs:** Team members communicated in Mandarin, English, Thai, Bahasa Malay, and more — information loss and translation errors occurred regularly
- **Manual task tracking:** Assignment and follow-up in the promotional approval process depended entirely on manual @-mentions and message forwarding, creating frequent breakdowns in accountability
- **Strict data sovereignty requirements:** As a global enterprise, internal data must satisfy rigorous data residency and security compliance standards that ruled out standard public cloud SaaS solutions

## COCO Solution

COCO deployed an AI employee directly inside the company's Lark workspace, with a **private deployment on GCP Singapore** to satisfy data sovereignty requirements — successfully passing a joint security review by three departments.

### Intent Recognition and Task Dispatch

- The AI monitors messages across multiple Lark groups in real time, automatically detecting approval requests, task instructions, information queries, and other structured intents
- Identified tasks are extracted, structured, and routed to the appropriate owner or team — no manual forwarding required

### Real-Time Multilingual Translation

- Messages are translated in real time across Mandarin, English, Thai, Bahasa Malay, Vietnamese, and other major Southeast Asian languages
- All team members receive communications in a consistent language, eliminating execution errors caused by translation ambiguity

### Promotional Approval Flow Automation

- Standardized approval workflows trigger automatically, including notifications, reminders, and archiving
- The full process is completed within Lark — no context switching to external systems required

## Key Results

- AI auto-dispatch replaced the previous manual group-relay coordination model, delivering a significant improvement in coordination efficiency
- Deployment cleared a **three-department joint security review**, satisfying all enterprise compliance requirements
- Cross-market information accuracy improved substantially, reducing rework caused by miscommunication
- Critical approval tasks are no longer lost in message streams — every workflow is traceable, nudgeable, and closed-loop

## Key Insight

> "For large enterprises, the biggest barrier to AI adoption isn't the technology — it's compliance. Private deployment that slots seamlessly into existing tools is the only path that actually lands."
