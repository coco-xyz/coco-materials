# Video Script: AI Knowledge Base Builder
Duration: ~30 seconds
Style: Warm yellow color tone, COCO branding

## Scene 1 (0-5s): Hook
[Fade in. Bold text on warm yellow background. Subtle particle animation.]
Voiceover: "We had a knowledge base with 340 articles. Sounds impressive until you look at the data: 127 articles hadn't been update"

## Scene 2 (5-12s): Pain Point
[Split screen: left side shows frustrated worker, right side shows mounting task list. Numbers animate upward.]
Voiceover: "Manual processes drain your team's time and energy. Hours lost every week on tasks that should take minutes."

## Scene 3 (12-22s): Solution
[COCO logo appears. Smooth transition to dashboard mockup. Feature bullets animate in one by one.]
Key features:
  - Analyzed 6 months of resolved tickets to identify the top 200 questions customers ask
  - Cross-referenced against our existing KB to find gaps and outdated content
  - Generated draft articles from resolved ticket solutions, formatted with step-by-step instructions, screenshots placeholders, and troubleshooting trees
Voiceover: "COCO's AI Knowledge Base Builder automates the workflow end-to-end. Set it up once, and watch it work."

## Scene 4 (22-28s): Results
[Impact metrics animate with counting effect. Green upward arrows.]
Voiceover: "KB articles: from 340 (127 outdated) to 520 (all current). Self-service deflection: from 12% to 41%. Support ticket volume: down 29%. The best support"

## Scene 5 (28-30s): CTA
[COCO logo centered. URL fades in below. Warm glow effect.]
Voiceover: "Visit coco.xyz"
