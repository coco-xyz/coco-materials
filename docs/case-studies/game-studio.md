# Independent Game Studio × COCO AI

> We're not adding AI tools. We're building an AI-native org structure.

## Customer Background

This studio is an independent mobile game developer with fewer than 10 team members, focused on developing and publishing mobile titles. Despite its small size, the team operates across a full business stack: game development, marketing, player community management, and data analytics. Team members have strong technical backgrounds and high expectations for AI — but found that scattered AI tools weren't delivering the compound benefits they anticipated.

## Challenges

- **AI tool silos:** Team members were using various AI tools independently (ChatGPT, Midjourney, Cursor, and others), but outputs couldn't be shared or accumulated across the team
- **The copy-paste tax:** Significant time was lost transferring outputs between AI tools or into documents — a constant, invisible overhead on every workflow
- **No shared memory:** Every AI conversation started from scratch. Tools had no awareness of the team's product context, player personas, or historical decisions — forcing redundant context-setting every session
- **Disconnected work tools:** Google Docs, Sheets, Calendar, and other workflow tools were completely siloed from the team's AI capabilities, keeping automation potential low

## COCO Solution

COCO helped the studio build an **Agentic OS** — a unified AI operating layer built on top of Google Workspace that gives every team member access to shared AI memory, context, and automation:

### Dev Assistant AI

- Connected to the code repository, technical documentation, and bug tracking systems for context-aware technical support
- Automatically drafts technical proposals, compiles development logs, and tracks feature progress
- Accumulates the team's product decisions and architectural choices so new members can quickly access historical context

### Marketing Growth AI

- Monitors industry trends, competitor launches, and player community sentiment to generate actionable market briefs
- Assists with copywriting, ASO (App Store Optimization) keyword research, and ad creative direction
- Integrates with Google Analytics to surface data-grounded marketing recommendations

### User Operations AI

- Aggregates player reviews, customer support tickets, and community discussions to extract user insights
- Generates user segmentation reports to inform version roadmap decisions
- Assists with player engagement on Discord and email channels to maintain community activity

## Key Results

- Team moved beyond AI tool silos — all AI outputs now flow into and accumulate within a unified platform
- Copy-paste tax dramatically reduced; team members focus on creative and strategic decisions rather than information transfer
- Shared AI memory established — context continuity across team members and time periods significantly improved
- Organizational shift completed: from a team that uses AI tools to a team that operates as an AI-native structure

## Key Insight

> "We're not adding AI tools for the team to use. We're redesigning how the organization operates. The AI employees are full members of this new structure — not plugins."
